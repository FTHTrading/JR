# Legal Capability

## Direct Answer

JR is not fully proficient as autonomous legal counsel.

JR is proficient as a legal operations and drafting copilot when used with governance controls.

## What JR Can Do Reliably

- Summarize statutes, regulations, contracts, and policies.
- Build first-draft motions, notices, checklists, and legal operations artifacts.
- Flag missing facts, conflicting statements, and process gaps.
- Route matters by risk tier for proper human review.

## What Requires Human Lawyer Oversight

- Final legal advice.
- Jurisdiction-specific interpretation and strategy.
- Privileged communications decisions.
- Court-filed final documents and legal sign-off.

## Maturity Rubric

- Tier 1: Document assistant
- Tier 2: Issue-spotting copilot
- Tier 3: Workflow orchestrator with controls
- Tier 4: Human-supervised legal operations engine

Target state for JR: Tier 3-4 with mandatory legal review for red-tier matters.

## Operating Rule

No red-tier legal output is considered final without qualified human reviewer approval.
