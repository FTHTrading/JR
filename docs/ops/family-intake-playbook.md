# Family Intake Playbook

## Mission

Serve families in urgent legal-adjacent crises by converting chaos into structured, attorney-ready case files.

## Hard Boundary

JR is a legal operations and advocacy support system, not autonomous legal counsel.

- JR can organize facts, timelines, records, and next-step options.
- Licensed attorneys must provide legal advice, strategy, and final legal sign-off on red-tier matters.

## Intake Pipeline

1. Capture inbound lead.
2. Assign urgency tier.
3. Build facts timeline.
4. Build evidence index.
5. Flag missing documents.
6. Route by escalation lane.
7. Deliver packet to advocate or attorney.

## Urgency Tiering

### Tier Red (Immediate)

- Child safety risk
- Active protective-order constraints affecting child needs
- Imminent homelessness or illegal lockout
- Criminal exposure with upcoming hearing
- Rights-interference allegations with potential evidence loss

SLA: First response within 15 minutes, packet in 2-6 hours.

### Tier Amber (Priority)

- Family, custody, or housing conflicts without immediate physical threat
- Criminal/civil issues needing structured pre-consult packet

SLA: First response within 2 hours, packet in 24 hours.

### Tier Green (Standard)

- General guidance, low-detail posts, non-urgent procedural questions

SLA: First response within 24 hours.

## Core Packet Structure

- Case Summary (1 page)
- Party Map
- Chronological Timeline
- Evidence Index
- Contradiction and Gap Flags
- Deadline Calendar
- Counsel Question List

## Escalation Lanes

- Family and Custody
- Criminal and Post-Conviction
- Housing and Eviction
- Civil Rights and Misconduct

## Quality Checks Before Handoff

- Facts are date-stamped where possible.
- Every claim has source evidence or marked as unverified.
- Missing evidence is clearly requested from client.
- No definitive legal advice language in JR output.
- Attorney review required for all red-tier packets.
