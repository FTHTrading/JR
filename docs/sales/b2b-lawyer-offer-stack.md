# B2B Lawyer Offer Stack

## Positioning

We turn disorganized inbound legal situations into structured, attorney-ready case packets before attorney review.

## Ideal Buyers

- Solo and small firms
- Family, criminal defense, housing, civil rights, and post-conviction practices
- Legal aid and advocacy organizations

## Offer Tiers

### Tier A: Intake Accelerator

- Lead triage and urgency scoring
- Structured intake normalization
- Basic timeline and document checklist

Deliverable time: same day

### Tier B: Case Packet Builder

- Full chronology
- Evidence index with source mapping
- Gap analysis and contradiction flags
- Counsel prep question set

Deliverable time: 24-48 hours

### Tier C: Crisis Response Lane

- Red-tier rapid intake
- Priority packet within hours
- Escalation-ready summary for counsel review

Deliverable time: 2-6 hours for first packet

## Pricing Model (Starter)

- Platform base: $299/month per office
- Active matter seat: $89/month per case manager
- Packet fees:
  - Intake Accelerator: $149 per matter
  - Case Packet Builder: $399 per matter
  - Crisis Response Lane: $699 per matter

## Value Proof Metrics

- Time-to-consult reduction
- Intake-to-retainer conversion lift
- Attorney admin time saved per matter
- Percentage of packets accepted without rework

## Objection Handling

- "Is this legal advice software?"
  - No. It is case infrastructure and workflow support.
- "Will this replace attorneys?"
  - No. It improves attorney leverage and reduces intake friction.
- "How do we control risk?"
  - Tiered escalation and mandatory human review for red-tier matters.
